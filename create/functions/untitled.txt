Mobile Media is Create’s award winning outreach strand. In 2009 MobileMedia celebrated 10 years of production work with young people acrossSwindon and Wiltshire funded by Grants for the Arts Lottery monies from ArtsCouncil England SW.
Our current ACE funded projects include
Media Academy: a range of courses and opportunities to support youngpeople in media production training for beginners through to Creative andMedia Diplomas and Apprenticeships.
YourFilm: A youth led group for young people who love all things media.YourFilm creates opportunities to gain experience on small commissions andmentor other young people through major projects like Mediabox.YourFilm has been led by the talented Sherylee Houssein for the last2 years…we lose her to the National Film and Television school in theAutumn…a testament to her determination and the great experience peoplegain through working with YourFilm and Mobile Media.
